### I3wm Information
- Distro: Arch Linux <br />
- wm: i3-gaps <br />
- Terminal: Alacritty <br />
- Alacritty-theme: Doomicide Darkocean <br />
- Background manager: Feh <br />
- Compositor: Picom <br />
- Drun/Program Launcher: Rofi <br />
- Audio: Pipewire

### Dependencies
- All of the above mentioned software (with distro of choice)
- betterlockscreen
- nm-applett
- pactl
- brightness
- rofi-theme-selector
- xss-lock
- xrandr
- arandr
- flameshot
- tlp
